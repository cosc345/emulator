LCC Health Manual

At the moment, all this app is doing is displaying sequences of number from 1 to 200 with caption that said 'Heart Rate'. If the number displays something less than 50 or greater than 200, a message will pop up that said 'Emergency, call 111 immediately'.

Loading Health app

1. Select the health app on selection tray.
2. Click load to load the app on to the screen.
3. Click clear to clear the screen.
